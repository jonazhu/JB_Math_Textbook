To say that solving the normal equations is mathematically equivalent to gradient-based optimization of the OLS

gradient descent and its variants including the Adam method [Sun et. al. 2019], matrix factorizations such as SV D
or QR [Aggarwal 2020], and mean-centering and standardizing data [Toth 2020].
